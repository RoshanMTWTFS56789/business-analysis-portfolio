# Universal Health Coverage SDG 3.8 Project

## Project Overview
This project analysed progress toward Universal Health Coverage under Sustainable Development Goal 3.8 using public international datasets.

The project focused on how UHC progress varied across regions and World Bank income groups, whether health expenditure was associated with stronger service coverage, and what recent progress suggested about resilience and indicative progress toward 2030.

## Business / Policy Question
How has progress toward Universal Health Coverage varied across world regions and income groups, and what factors distinguish countries making faster or slower progress?

## Key Insight
UHC progress improved between 2000 and 2022, but progress was uneven across regions, income groups and country trajectories. Higher spending was generally associated with stronger service coverage, but spending alone did not guarantee better outcomes. Financial protection and health-system resilience remained important parts of the UHC challenge.
